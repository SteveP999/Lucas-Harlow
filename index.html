About the Artist

Lucas Harlow

Lucas Harlow composes music that exists between silence and sound — where a single piano note can hold an entire afternoon. His debut album Daydreams is an invitation to slow down, breathe, and let the mind wander somewhere beautiful. A Hello Texas Records artist.
